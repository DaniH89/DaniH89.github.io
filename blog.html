Give me blogging, I'll show you passion...

After encountering the reality of Modern Day Slavery, known today as Human Trafficking, and exhausting all other ways to help, blogging became a suitable solution when all previous efforts failed. Being a college student, money was not within reach. Instead, I chose awareness. My Blog is called Human Traffricking our Moder Day Slavery and its purpose is to serve the voiceless. The child that crys out for justice in a world that's so busy to stop and listen.

A. Call. To. Action.

Find an organization in your area and get involved today!

If you’re a graduating college student like me, then you probably aren't making $100,000 right out school. For me, money isn’t readily available and because of this known fact, I would find myself at a crossroad. Having a desire to help and funds being at an all time low was not ideal. Then I had an epiphany; I can get involved with an organization and help that way. Now I know you’re probably thinking “Duh, get involved,” but for me with a need this big, I saw giving as the best way to help stop this repulsive pandemic. However, spreading awareness and getting educated was the best place to start. From there, I researched organizations and found one that was perfect on for me and got involved.
